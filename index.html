About

Ethical

I will only ever charge you either a fixed price for a month's work or an hourly rate for my time and knowledge. I won't add charges for small changes that can be done quickly.

Human

We're human beings, not drones. No suits, no pretentious manner, just a hard-working guy who's willing to help.

Honest

If something takes a lot less time than initially planned, I'll tell you and the bill will go down. I'll also suggest changes in design where applicable.

Focused

If I am working on your project then that's all I will be doing. Your project won't get delayed just because I have other responsibilities.

Who am I?

I'm a freelance developer currently living in the UK countryside. I work with HTML, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript and Vue primarily and develop websites, apps and enjoy the learning involved when thrown a new challenge. If you have a project you want some help with, or you have a project that's not even been born, then get in touch below.
